ğŸ’¡ How to Check Your Skin Type

Step 1: Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and pat dry.

Step 2: Leave your skin bare (no moisturizer or product) for 1 hour.

Step 3: After an hour, observe:

  • Oily Skin: Shiny on forehead, nose, and cheeks.
  • Dry Skin: Feels tight, flaky, or rough.
  • Combination Skin: Oily T-zone (forehead/nose/chin) but dry cheeks.
  • Normal Skin: Feels balanced, not too oily or dry.
  • Sensitive Skin: Redness or irritation after cleansing.
